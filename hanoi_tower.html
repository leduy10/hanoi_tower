Hanoi Tower in Dart

  • Difficulty
Canvas is not supported in your browser.

To win the game, move the entire stack of discs to the third tower while respecting those rules:
1) You can only move one disc at a time
2) You can only move the upper disc of a tower
3) You can only place a disc on an empty tower or on a bigger disc

To move a disc:
1) Click on the disc you wish to move
2) Click on the base of the tower where you wish to move the disc
